About Me

Long before the First Age, I was originally a Maia of the Vala Aulë; My name was Mairon. I gave knowledge and encouragement in forging of the Rings of Power, though in secret I forged my own, the One Ring, to rule them all. During the Battle of Dagorlad, Isildur, son of Elendil, took up his father's broken sword, and cut the One Ring from my finger. Despite my defeat, I seek the One Ring to defeat men and rule Middle Earth...
